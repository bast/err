Halloumi

Prepare marinade

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Chili flakes
  • Juice from a quarter of a lemon

Instructions

  • Marinade
  • Fry for 2 minutes on each side
  • Decorate with spring onions
