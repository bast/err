Baked cod

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp melted butter (unsalted)
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Red paprika on top

Instructions

  • 220 C
  • 15 min
